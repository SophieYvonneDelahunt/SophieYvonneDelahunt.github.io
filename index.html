A Steel Path to the Sky

2024 - Present
Science Fiction

A Steel Path to the Sky

At the dawn of the twenty-third century, the world is unrecognisable. In the wake of societal collapse and a global mass extinction event, human civilisation has risen from the ashes of the old world with a focus on sustainability and social harmony.

Seventeen-year-old Taia Sinclair stares up at the cold Vancouver sky on clear nights from the roof of her residential tower, watching spacecraft pass overhead. She dreams of travelling to the stars. Yet her legs—gripped by the rigid steel of a medical exoskeleton—hold her fast. Plagued by a terminal illness, Taia knows that she’ll never live to set foot on another world. Her dream is unattainable. But in her heart, she refuses to let go.

Elsewhere in the megacity, a man tinkers with cybernetics—a technological dead end. A heavy lift freighter rises into the skies, its crew ignorant of the critical fault in its engines. An eldritch monster forged of circuitry and code plots its escape. These four paths will come to intersect… and Taia doesn’t yet know that her journey is about to begin—one that will see her face questions on what it means to be alive, on where lie the boundaries of humanity, and how much she is willing to sacrifice to see her fervent dream realised.

Aria of a Falling Sky

2017 - 2024
Science Fantasy

Aria of a Falling Sky

Asciydria Tower—the ceiling of the world. Broken by time and conflict, it stands as a memorial to its own bloody history. The spire’s upper flanks glitter with ice sheets, its lower reaches swallowed by forests. Beneath the pine boughs, humans gather around their hearths and tell stories, while among the clouds, the nomads of the Sarra clan walk the tower’s myriad paths. And under the silent hymns of the sky, its bones continue to rot.

The tower will fall—soon.

Yet a child awakens in the depths of the machine, carrying the weight of the world itself. Can this young android and her sarcastic AI companion climb to the top of the tower in time to save it, or will it all come crashing down around them? Follow Sylphira on her journey to save Asciydria Tower, and learn what it means to be a Tower Administrator.
